Adelaide Metro GTFS+ Release Notes

Release: 09-12-2013


The following is a list of changes from the previous GTFS feed:


Special Events:

None.

Detours:

None.

Service Updates:

1) The Noarlunga train line will be closed on weeknights and all day on weekends. There are N3 and N4 substitue bus services for passengers. More information can be found here: http://www.adelaidemetro.com.au/Announcements/Service-updates/Noarlunga-line-open-planned-works-late-night-and-weekends-substitute-bus-timetable

General Amendments:

1) During the December and January school holidays selected G40 services do not travel to/from Flinders Uni. We are now reflecting this in our GTFS feed. You can find specific details about which services are affected on downloadable PDF timetable: http://www.adelaidemetro.com.au/routes/G40
2) Due to the above amendment to the G40 services, there is a new route G40C.


Additonal notes:

Please note that we have changed the URL for our GTFS feeds. From now on our feeds can be downloaded from this location:

http://adelaidemetro.com.au/GTFS/google_transit.zip

From Mon 2 Dec, the feed will only be available from this new location. We recommend redirecting any automated downloads you may have in place for your applications.

Additionally, the feed can be downloaded from the Adelaide Metro website via the Developer Information page: http://www.adelaidemetro.com.au/Developer-Information
