Detours:

None.

Service Updates:

1) On Australia Day (January 26) there is a unique tram timetable. The Noarlunga train line will also be operational - please note that the Noarlunga substitute bus services will not be operating on this day.

2) The first service change of 2014 will happen on Monday January 27, 2014. This service change does NOT impact trains, Outer South and Outer North bus services. Here is a complete list of services which are changing:
