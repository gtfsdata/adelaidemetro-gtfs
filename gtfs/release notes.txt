Adelaide Metro GTFS Release Notes

The following is a list of changes and upcoming events:


Upcoming Public Holidays:

12 March 2018 - Adelaide Cup
30 March 2018 - Good Friday
02 April 2018 - Easter Monday
25 April 2018 - ANZAC Day


Special Events:

1) Tram night closures - February 8-14 and 19-22.

General Amendments:

2) Implemented night closures for the Outer Harbor and Grange train lines including substitute bus services W1 and H1.
3) Implemented Tram night closure timetable for February 19-22.
4) Stop 15 Churchill Rd - West side has had its stop coordinates changed.
