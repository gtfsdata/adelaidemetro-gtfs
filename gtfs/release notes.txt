Service Updates:

1) December 26-29 the Belair train line will be closed. Sub buses are in operation for the duration of the closure, please see www.adelaidemetro.com.au for further information.
2) December 27-28 the Seaford train line will have night closures. Sub buses will be in operation, please see www.adelaidemetro.com.au for more information. Please note that January Seaford closures information will be added to the GTFS at a later date.
3) December 29-31 there will be reduced services across bus, train and tram. Special timetables are in place for the duration. Please see www.adelaidemetro.com.au for further information. Please note that data for December 31 will be added to the GTFS some time next week.

General Amendments:

1) Some Tonsley Trains that are in operation 29-30 December were incorrectly labeled as Seaford trips. This has now been corrected.
