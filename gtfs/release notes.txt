Adelaide Metro GTFS Release Notes

The following is a list of changes and upcoming events:


Upcoming Public Holidays:


None.


General Amendments:

1) Stop 27 Main South Rd - North West side is no longer serviced by routes T721 and T722.

2) New tram timetables have been implemented from July 29, 2018. Due to the extension of our tram network, routes are now labeled as FESTVL, GLNELG and BTANIC. Please see www.adelaidemetro.com.au for more information.

3) Route 97A will be decommissioned from July 29 due to the tram extension opening.
