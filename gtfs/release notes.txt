Adelaide Metro GTFS Release Notes

The following is a list of changes and upcoming events:


Upcoming Public Holidays:


Labour Day - October 1


General Amendments:


1) Added services for the Royal Adelaide Show (August 31 - 09 September)

- 21W is the city shuttle service
- 22W is the O-Bahn shuttle service
- SHOWC is a train service traveling between Adelaide and Goodwood (via the Showgrounds)
- All Seaford trains will service the Adelaide Showground station after 8:30am until last service, with the exception of selected peak services.

2) For the duration of the Royal Adelaide Show, all normal bus routes that service Stop V1 Currie St will be redirected to Stop V2 Currie St. Stop V1 Currie St is utilised exclusively by the 21W.
