General Amendments:

1) General amendments have been made to all O-Bahn buses (routes in the 500 series, C1, C2, M44 and G40). Most O-Bahn buses will service different stops in the city, at Paradise Interchange and at Tea Tree Plaza depending on the time of day. There are also rules in place for buses to not service Stop 2 Hackney Rd between 8am-9:15am and 4:30pm-6:00pm on weekdays.
