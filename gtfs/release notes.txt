Adelaide Metro GTFS Release Notes

The following is a list of changes and upcoming events:


Upcoming Public Holidays:


None.


General Amendments:


1) Routes 167 and 168 now service stops T2 Grenfell St, and V3 and X1 Currie St when leaving the city.

2) Routes 115, 117 and 118 now service stops V1, W1 and X1 Currie St when leaving the city.

3) All Light City Buses routes have been moved to the Torrens Transit agency.

4) GLNELG and FESTVL tram routes will not be operating through Jetty Rd on 21 October due to a street party.
