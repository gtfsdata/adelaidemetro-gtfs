Service Updates:

1) Additional Before Dawn services will be in operation for ANZAC Day on April 25th.

2) School Holidays are between April 13th-April 24th. Some trips have been "turned off" for this period as they only operate on school days only. Some trips will also operate during school holiday periods only.

3) Trip data for AFL Rounds 6-17 has been added to the GTFS.


General Amendments:

1) Previously it was advised the tram and J1X timetables would change on April 26th. These changes will no longer occur. Data has been reverted back to "as is" for these services.

2) Updates have been made to route 744. Parts of the route were servicing stops on the incorrect side of the road.

3) Stop 7 South Road (Thebarton) no longer exsists on both sides of the road. Stop removed from route B10, no other services affected.
