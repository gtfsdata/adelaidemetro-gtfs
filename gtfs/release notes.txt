Adelaide Metro GTFS Release Notes

The following is a list of changes and upcoming events:


Upcoming Public Holidays:

March 9, 2015 - Adelaide Cup


Special Events:

1) Fringe Opening Parade on Friday, Febraury 13th. This affects the tram line.


Detours:

None.


Service Updates:

1) From 12th February until 15th March, additional 99A & 99C trips will be in operation due to the Fringe.

2) Unique tram timetable for 13th February due to the Fringe opening festival.

3) On 16th February the Seaford and Tonsley train lines will have a new timetable.

4) On 16th February, bus routes on the 741-750-751-752-755-756 timetable will have updated trip times.



General Amendments:

1) Stop 27 Sturt Rd - North side removed from routes servicing Flinders Medical Centre. This is due to these routes being unable to service the stop and safely make the turn to the Medical Centre. Affected routes are 101, 300, 300H, 300J, 320, 600, 600B, 601, 720H, G10 and N10. Please note only one N10 trip services Flinders, the other trips will service Stop 27.

2) Updates to trip times for outbound (away from city) 253X.

3) C1 and C2 have been updated to reflect propper stopping patterns on weekdays. This includes not servicing stops along The Grove Way and Golden Grove Rd, and Stop 2 Hackney Rd rules.

4) Updates to trip times for 300U services departing Glenelg. Trips were approx. two mins out from their correct departure time.

5) 178 trips departing from Stop 26 Montacute Rd to the City have been added to the GTFS.

6) Updates have been made to Gawler Central Station stop location. This applies to the inbound (to city) stop only.
