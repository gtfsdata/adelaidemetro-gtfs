Special Events:

1) On March 22 the Rolling Stones will be performing at Adelaide Oval. There will be several "AO" and "AOX" bus routes in operation for the event. More information is available at http://www.adelaidemetro.com.au/Announcements/Events/Special-public-transport-to-Rolling-Stones-Concert.

Detours:

None.

Service Updates:

1) We have implemented the Adelaide Oval Footy Express bus services for this year's AFL matches at Adelaide Oval. These run to a unique timetable each week. We currently have trips for up to Round 5, we will release trip data for more rounds in the coming weeks. More information is available at http://adelaidemetro.com.au/Announcements/Events/Adelaide-Oval-Footy-Express.

General Amendments:

1) Stop 99A Aldinga Beach Rd added to routes 751, 755 and 756.
